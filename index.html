Luke Bryan

What Makes You Country

Thomas Luther "Luke" Bryan is a country music singer-songwriter.

Here's a short time line of Luke Bryan's life:

  • 1976 - 17th of July. Born in rural Leesburg, Georgia.
  • 1995 - His brother died.
  • 1999 - Graduated from the Sigma Chi fraternity.
  • 2006 - 8th of December. Married to Caroline Boyer.
  • 2007 - His sister died.
  • 2007 - August. Released debut album I'll Stay Me with Capitol Records.
  • 2009 - 10th of March. Released an EP named Spring Break.
  • 2009 - October. Released his second album, Doin' My Thing.
  • 2011 - 9th of August. Released his third studio album, Tailgates & Tanlines.
  • 2013 - 12th of August. Released his fourth album.
  • 2014 - March. Bryan came up with his sixth EP, "Spring Break 6...Like We Ain't Ever"
  • 2015 - May. Bryan came up with his fifth studio album, Kill the Lights, "Kick the Dust Up".
  • 2016 - Bryan was one of the 30 artists who performed on "Forever Country" a mash-up track of "Take Me Home, Country Roads", "On the Road Again" and "I Will Always Love You".
  • 2017 - February. Luke Bryan performed the National Anthem at Super Bowl LI at NRG Stadium in Houston, TX.
  • 2017 - 8th of December. Bryan released his sixth album What Makes You Country.
